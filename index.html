Our Medical Services

General OPD

Comprehensive outpatient care providing routine medical consultations for both adults and children. Our experienced healthcare professionals deliver thorough evaluations, accurate diagnoses, and personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient’s needs. We focus on preventive care, early detection of illnesses, and continuous health management to ensure long-term well-being in a comfortable and patient-centered environment.

Maternal & Child Care

We provide comprehensive, patient-centered maternal and pediatric healthcare services designed to support both mothers and children at every stage of development. Our care includes thorough assessment, accurate diagnosis, and effective treatment of a wide range of acute and chronic medical conditions.

Diagnostic Laboratory & Imaging Services

Accredited diagnostic laboratory providing accurate and reliable medical testing and imaging services to support effective clinical diagnosis and treatment.

  • Accredited diagnostic laboratory services
  • Echocardiography (Heart Ultrasound)
  • Digital radiographic (X-ray) services
  • Routine and specialized laboratory investigations

Chronic Disease

Ongoing, patient-centered care for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and other chronic conditions. We focus on regular monitoring, effective treatment, and lifestyle guidance to help patients control their illness and improve overall quality of life.

Medical Evaluation for Travelers Abroad

Comprehensive pre-travel and pre-employment medical screening for patients traveling abroad, including Gulf countries, with full medical clearance and documentation support.

Credit Medical Services & Institutional Partnerships

We provide credit-based medical services for employees of government institutions and non-governmental organizations, ensuring accessible and flexible healthcare support.

Our clinic proudly collaborates with recognized institutions and organizations to facilitate streamlined medical care for their staff and beneficiaries.

  • Ethiopian Postal Service (ELPA)
  • Ethio Telecom
  • Ethiopian Airlines

Emergency Care

Immediate medical intervention for emergencies with qualified staff on-site.
